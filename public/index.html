Frequently Asked Questions

Will my CPA/accountant accept this?

Absolutely! Our export format (PDF summary + CSV + original images) is exactly what CPAs request. Many accountants actually recommend us to their clients.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, secure cloud storage, and never sell your data. Your receipts are backed up automatically and accessible only to you.

What if I get audited by the IRS?

You're covered. Our system keeps receipts for 7 years (IRS requirement) and exports them in audit-ready format. Several users have successfully used Reciptera during audits.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel with one click. No questions asked. Plus, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

Do you integrate with QuickBooks or Xero?

Our CSV export works seamlessly with QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and any accounting software. Direct integrations coming soon.

Is this for businesses or individuals?

Reciptera is designed for US freelancers, contractors, and self-employed individuals who file Schedule C. Not for corporations or complex business accounting.
